# Roadmap — From v0 to the Full Room

*Statuses are honest. Frontiers are labeled. Every milestone is
forkable — nobody needs permission to move one.*

## v0 — The Harvest Room  ✅ BUILDABLE TODAY
Panel → banks → copper. Off-the-shelf, ~$300–450, one afternoon.
Guide: `BUILD_GUIDE.md`. Ledger: `power_budget.py`.
**Milestone: first-light photos posted as issues.**

## v0.1 — The Maker Audio Build  🔧 PARTS EXIST
Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W + Shairport-Sync + DAC + wired cans: an AirPlay
lossless headphone rig, fed from a buffer cell. Flywheel math in
`AUDIO.md`. **Milestone: first working rig with power logs.**

## v0.5 — The Dongle Bridge  ✅ BUYABLE TODAY
Uncompressed-PCM gaming headset + USB-C dongle: WAV over air now,
gaming tuning accepted. **Milestone: measured latency + battery data
posted.**

## v1.0 — The Beam Tier  🚧 THE FRONTIER
IR emitter hub + PV receiver strips. Specs: PDF Sections 4–5. Safety:
non-negotiable (Class 1, <10 ms interlock — `safety_interlock.ino`).
Paths: license Wi-Charge-class art, or clean-sheet. PDF targets:
**prototype ~90 days from detailed-design start; certification ~12
months.** **Milestone: one emitter + one strip trickle-charging one
headphone, on video.**

## v1.5 — The Perpetual Headphone  🔭 INTEGRATION
Micro-power Wi-Fi audio (XPAN-class, but open) + receiver strip +
buffer cell inside audiophile cans. Every part exists; nobody has
bolted them together. **Milestone: a headphone that never meets a
charger, seven days logged.**

## v2.0 — The Night Shift  🔬 YOUNG SCIENCE
Sky-facing radiative panels: cooling by night, whisper-tier harvest,
winter-optimized. Physics: `NIGHT_SHIFT.md`. **Milestone: measured
below-ambient panel data from a humid-climate site.**

## v3.0 — The Photon Building  🌅 THE HORIZON
Rooms become floors; sky-cooled condensers exhaust heat off-planet;
new construction faces the sun by day and the stars by night.

## How To Move a Milestone
Build it, measure it, post it — an issue here or a fork anywhere.
No gatekeeper exists. That's the point.
